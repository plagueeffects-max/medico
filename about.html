ABOUT MEDICO
SCIENTIFIC SERVICE CENTRE

Since its establishment in 2000, Medico Scientific Service Centre has grown into one of the premier and most trusted suppliers of medical and scientific equipment in Jammu & Kashmir. For 25 years, we have been supporting hospitals, clinics, and laboratories with world-class products and excellent services, ensuring that healthcare professionals have access to the tools they need to save lives.

Mr. Syed Naseer Ahmad Andrabi

Visionary Founder & Proprietor

Mr. Syed Naseer Ahmad Andrabi, the visionary founder of Medico Scientific Service Centre, began this journey in the year 2000 with a strong commitment to provide the best medical equipment and services to the healthcare sector of Jammu & Kashmir. With extensive experience, Mr. Andrabi has guided the company with integrity, dedication, and professionalism. Under his leadership, Medico Scientific Service Centre has become a trusted name that stands for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. His belief has always been simple: "Only the best solutions are good enough for saving lives."
